Pancakes

Preparation Time: 15 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add milk and eggs
  3. Mix until smooth batter
  4. Pour batter on pan
  5. Cook both sides and serve

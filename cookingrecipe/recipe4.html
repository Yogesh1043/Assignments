Burger

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the patty
  2. Toast burger buns
  3. Add lettuce and patty
  4. Add cheese and sauce
  5. Close bun and serve

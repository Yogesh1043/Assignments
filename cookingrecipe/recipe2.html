Chicken Biryani

Preparation Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani masala
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices
  3. Fry onions until golden brown
  4. Cook chicken with spices
  5. Add rice and cook until done
  6. Serve hot with raita

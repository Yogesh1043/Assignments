Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add eggs and butter
  3. Mix into smooth batter
  4. Bake in oven 30 mins
  5. Cool and serve
